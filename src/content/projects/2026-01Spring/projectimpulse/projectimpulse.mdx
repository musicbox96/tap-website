desc : Project Impulse is a compact spring-powered jumping robot designed to simulate a human foot-like launch mechanism. The robot uses a winch system to compress high-force springs and releases stored energy to perform controlled vertical jumps. The structure is 3D printed and guided by linear rails to ensure stable motion. The system is powered by a LiPo battery and controlled using a Raspberry Pi Pico. A high-current BTS7960 motor driver controls the DC gear motor used for compressing the springs. A buck converter steps down voltage to safely power the microcontroller. Other features like AI controller(using Raspberry Pi 2W),moving forward(using servo motor) and turning (using 55mm ducted fan) will be added later.
